The Oxira Story

Oxira is the premium packaged drinking water brand introduced by Divera Beverages. Situated in the heart of Surat at Gothan Tilavat Bridge, our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is dedicated to a single mission: delivering unmatched purity.

We pride ourselves on our rigorous quality control and advanced purification processes, resulting in our esteemed BIS Certification.
