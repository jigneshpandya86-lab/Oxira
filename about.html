Purity in Every Drop

Oxira, a brand of Divera Beverages, was founded with a simple mission: to provide pure, safe, and affordable packaged drinking water to every household and business in Gujarat.

Based in Surat, we've built our reputation on unwavering quality standards, modern manufacturing practices, and a genuine commitment to customer satisfaction.

From our state-of-the-art facility at Gothan, we serve thousands of satisfied customers across Gujarat with both 200ml and 500ml BIS certified packaged drinking water.
